Trivia Game Rules

1. Game Setup

  • 2-6 players can participate
  • Each player chooses a unique color token
  • Place all tokens on the center "Hub" space

2. Gameplay

  • Players take turns rolling the die and moving their token
  • Answer a question in the category matching the space color
  • Correct answers allow another turn
  • Incorrect answers end your turn

3. Winning the Game

  • Collect one wedge of each category color
  • Return to the center "Hub" space
  • Correctly answer a final question in a category of the other players' choosing

4. Special Spaces

  • Roll Again: Take another turn
  • Hub: Choose any category when landing here
  • Category Headquarters: Earn a wedge if you answer correctly
